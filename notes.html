Lecture Notes

These (unofficial) notes are based on modules taught at UCL. The "essentials" pdf contains only examinable theorems, definitions and proofs, while the "full pdf" contains everything mentioned in the lectures (and more). What is examinable in my year may not be in yours so use with caution. Corrections are welcome by email.

Year 2

  • Complex Analysis (MATH0013) (full), (essentials)
    Holomorphic functions, Cauchy's Theorem, Meromorphic functions (and more). Following Stein–Shakarchi Vol. 2 and Ahlfors.
  • Further linear algebra (MATH0014)
    Polynomial rings, linear operators and Jordan normal form, bilinear forms and inner product spaces. Lots of extras. Following Axler, Hoffman–Kunze, Roman, Halmos.
  • Fluid Mechanics (MATH0015)
    2 dimensional motion, dynamics, surface waves.
  • Methods 3 (MATH0016)
    Introductory fourier series, calculus of variations, PDEs, vector calculus.
  • Curves and Surfaces (MATH0020)
    Classical differential geometry in $\mathbb{R}^3$. 1st and 2nd fundamental form, Theorema Egregium and Gauss Bonnet. Following Do Carmo.
  • Metric Spaces (MATH0051)
    Introductory ideas in metric and topological spaces. Lots of extra topology. Following Rudin, Sutherland and Munkres.
  • Groups and Rings (MATH0053)
    Standard results in finite group theory and ring theory (with free use of categorical language). Following Aluffi, Lang, Fraleigh.
  • Methods 4 (MATH0056)
    Separation of variables in cylindrical/spherical coordinates and Laplace/Fourier transform methods for PDEs.
